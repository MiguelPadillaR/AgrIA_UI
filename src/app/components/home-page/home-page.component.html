Quick Start

This is a quick guide to get you started with our service. Follow these simple steps to find and analyze your parcels.

  1. Navigate to the Parcel Finder.
  2. Enter the cadastral reference and select a date.
  3. Click 'Find Parcel' to retrieve information.
  4. Review the parcel details and confirm.
